Taco Bell/KFC

As a student with no car (and no money), this place is perfect. I always get a crunchwrap with a baja blast whenever I go, or if I feel like it, a chicken sandwich. 9/10

RATING: 9/10

Subway

A classic. The closest one is an 11 minute drive, but DoorDash is always an option. Making your own sandwich in the dining hall may be a better option money-wise, but it just isn't the same!

RATING: 7/10

Fuji Restaurant

Two words: SO. GOOD. I ate a teriyaki chicken bento box here and it was really good. It is a 4 minute drive or a 10 minute walk from campus. You get your money's worth for sure. Shoutout to Coach Brandon for the great food suggestion. Will be returning!

RATING: 10/10

Baja Box

By far the best place I have ever gone for tacos. It's a great location right on the beach and conveniently close to campus. It is $13-$15 for 2 tacos, but SO worth it. Their ice cream is also good!

RATING: 100/10
